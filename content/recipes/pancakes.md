# Ingredients

- $\frac12$ c. flour
- 3 $\frac12$ tsp. baking powder
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 Tbs. sugar
- 1 $\frac14$ c. milk
- 1 egg
- 3 Tbs. melted butter

# Directions
1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Mix in wet ingredients until mostly incorporated
3. Let sit for ~5 minutes
4. Cook on griddle or pan
