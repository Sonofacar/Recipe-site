# Ingredients
- 1/3 c. butter, softened
- 3 c. powdered sugar
- 8 oz. cream cheese, softened

# Directions
1. Mix ingredients in a bowl (usually stand mixer)
2. Beat at high speed until fluffy (~5-7 minutes)
