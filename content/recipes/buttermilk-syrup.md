# Ingredients
- 1 c. sugar
- 1/2 c. buttermilk
- 1/2 c. butter
- 1 tsp. baking soda
- 1 tsp. vanilla extract

# Directions
1. Heat sugar, buttermilk, and butter in saucepan until it boils.
2. Remove from heat and stir in baking soda and vanilla.
